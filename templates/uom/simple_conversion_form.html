Quick Help
Workflow Examples:
Same Unit (Simplest):
  • Paint: Buy 10L → Store 10L → Sell 10L
  • Screws: Buy 100 pcs → Store 100 pcs → Sell 100 pcs
Trading:
  • Steel Rods: Buy 1M → Store 1M → Sell 4 pieces
  • Nuts & Bolts: Buy 1kg → Store 1kg → Sell 100 pieces
Production Materials:
  • Steel Sheet: Buy in Kg → BOM uses in sq.m → Makes castor wheels
  • Steel Rod: Buy in Meters → BOM uses in mm → Makes axles
  • Rubber: Buy in Kg → BOM uses in grams → Makes wheel coating
Tip: Production materials won't be sold directly. They'll be consumed during manufacturing to create finished products that you sell to customers.
